## The 30-Second Read

**Table 1**: Every funded governance company is single-purpose. t54 Labs = identity only. Geordie AI = security only. Truth Systems = compliance only. Trace = orchestration only. None have a marketplace, economic system, or conservation law. Signomy is the only one with all six layers: **identity + trust + governance + provenance + marketplace + economics**.

**Table 2**: Microsoft validates the space with an open-source toolkit but commoditizes components, not systems. Fetch.ai has distribution (2.8M agents) but no governance. Neither has a constitutional framework.

**Table 3**: Every solo-founder marketplace is single-purpose bounties. Moltbook proved viral adoption is possible but had zero governance and zero monetization — Meta bought the team, not the product. CIVITAE has the deepest architecture of any company in this group.

**The gap nobody is filling**: t54 Labs has identity. Geordie has security. Microsoft has a toolkit. None of them have a constitutional governance protocol with a conservation law, a provenance chain, a governed marketplace, an economic system, and a sovereign entity owning it all. That is the white space.
